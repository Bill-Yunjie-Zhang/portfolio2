With different kinds of restaurants all around our campus, students in my school tend to waste a lot of time trying to find their best bet in the neighborhood. C.A.T.E. is a student group that focuses on giving our fellow students at Chengdu No.7 High convenience while choosing and finding a suitable restaurant.
We started by letting our field agents evaluating restaurants adjacent to our campus. Then the field agents are also responsible for article writing that will be presented on the moment of QQ (a social networking app that is commonly used among students). We also strike deals with restaurants, such as giving discounts to students in our school.
